<p align="center">

Supala enables users to collateralize tokenized equities such as **AAPL, AMZN, and MSFT**, borrow against them across chains, and **actively trade their collateral without closing their lending position**.

> üí° **Key Insight**  
> Supala introduces **Tradeable Collateral**, transforming lending positions from static vaults into **dynamic, actively managed portfolios**.

By combining Mantle‚Äôs **RWA-first ecosystem** with LayerZero‚Äôs **omnichain messaging**, Supala turns tokenized stocks into **fully composable cross-chain financial building blocks**.

## Supala‚Äôs Role in Advancing the Mantle Ecosystem

### Expanding Mantle‚Äôs RWA Utility
Supala transforms tokenized stocks on Mantle from passive representations into productive, on-chain collateral. By enabling borrowing, leverage, and active collateral management, Supala increases real economic activity and capital efficiency for RWAs within the Mantle ecosystem.
